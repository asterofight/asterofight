Astero Fight - Privacy Policy

Free MMO space shooter by many of us

As Anonymous

This site does not collect any data and it does not use cookies.

Your name can be anything you like and should not be your real name. Nevertheless we do not store the name either.

Logged In

Login token is stored on your computer to automatically log in each time you open the game.

Your name/email/password hash are stored on the server for login purposes but are not used otherwise.
